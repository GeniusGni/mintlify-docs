## Create from a template

_<u>Estimated time: 1 minute</u>_

If you are unsure where to start, templates make it easy to get going right away. Just choose a template and customize it to fit your needs.

Follow the steps in this guide to create a delicate PPT of Starbucks:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project from a template">
    Click the **\+** as shown in the following image, then choose **Create Project**:

    ![image-5](/images/image-5.png)

    Choose **PPT:Corporate....**

    ![Templates](/images/templates.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview the template before creating">
    By clicking preview botton, you can browse the template:

    ![Previewtemplate](/images/previewtemplate.png)

    ![Previewdetail](/images/previewdetail.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revising with agent">
    By communicating with the agent, the content within the template is transformed into what you need, ultimately becoming your unique app.
  </Step>
</Steps>
